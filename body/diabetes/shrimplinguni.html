FRESH HERB SHRIMP LINGUINI

This delightful dish combines shrimp with linguini pasta and a medley of herbs and spices. Fresh Herb Shrimp Linguini earns its diabetes-friendly badge through its mindful combination of ingredients. Shrimp, a protein-packed ingredient, offers satisfaction without causing significant spikes in blood sugar. Linguini, when enjoyed in moderation, provides a balanced dose of carbohydrates. The infusion of fresh herbs not only elevates the taste but also brings a nutritional boost without excess calories or carbs. With its meticulous balance of protein, carbs, and wholesome herbs, this dish stands as a health-conscious choice for a diabetes-conscious diet.

INGREDIENTS:

  • ½ kilogram peeled shrimp
  • ¼ to ½ kilogram linguini pasta
  • ¼ cup Parmesan cheese
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons rosemary
  • Salt and pepper

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil and cook pasta for 8-10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, saute the garlic and shrimp with olive oil. Stir occasionally until shrimp is cooked.
  3. Add half of the Parmesan cheese, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Stir for 30 seconds and add the cooked pasta.
  4. Transfer to a clean plate and sprinkle the remaining cheese and rosemary.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  320kcal
Carbohydrates  33g
Protein  27g
Fat 10g
Sodium 1120.3mg
Sugar 0.8g
