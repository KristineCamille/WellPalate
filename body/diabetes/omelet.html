MUSHROOM OMELET

Indulge in the delicious simplicity of a mushroom omelet—a tasty blend of mushrooms and eggs. It's a smart choice for those watching their blood sugar levels, as mushrooms bring a savory depth without spiking blood sugar. Paired with protein from eggs, this omelet is not just satisfying but also nutritious. Enjoy a wholesome and easy meal with this recipe.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 teaspoons unsalted butter
  • 1 can of sliced mushroom
  • 1-2 garlic cloves
  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2-3 green onions
  • 2 teaspoon parsely

PROCEDURES:

  1. Melt the butter in a medium frying pan over medium heat. Add the garlic and mushrooms. Stir for 3-4 minutes or until mushrooms are beginning to brown. Once cooked, remove from the pan and set aside.
  2. Whisk the egg, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the remaining butter to the frying pan and add the green onions and cook, stirring, for 30 seconds. Spread the green shallots over the base of the frying pan.
  4. Add the egg mixture to the pan and swirl to coat the bottom of the pan and cover the green shallots. Sprinkle with the parsley. Cover with a lid or a piece of foil and cook over medium heat for 2 minutes or until just set.
  5. Spoon the mushrooms over one half of the omelet and gently shake the omelet onto a serving plate, flipping the other half over to cover the mushrooms. Serve immediately with bread and a crisp green salad, if desired.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  305cal
Carbohydrates  4.6g
Protein  20g
Fat 23g
Sodium 616mg
Sugar 2.2 g
