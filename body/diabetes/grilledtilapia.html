GRILLED TILAPIA

Grilled tilapia, prepared with a health-conscious touch, offers a delightful combination of flavors. The fish is seasoned and grilled to perfection that gives way to tender and flavorful dish. This not only imparts a savory and delicious taste but also aligns with dietary considerations. It's not just a meal; it's a celebration of your health journey, proving that you can relish the rich flavors of Filipino cuisine while making mindful choices. Get ready to indulge in the goodness of grilled tilapia—because taking care of your health never tasted this good!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1-2 pieces of tilapia
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • Calamansi
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooking oil

PROCEDURES:

  1. Combine the tomatoes, onion, salt, pepper, and calamansi juice in a bowl.
  2. Stuff the mixture into the tilapia.
  3. Brush oil and salt on the outer part of tilapia. You may wrap the fish in aluminum foil and grill it or you may grill it over a pan, just use a cover for air circulation.
  4. Grill the tilapia for 10 minutes per side
  5. Remove the aluminum foil and serve the tilapia with soy sauce, onion, and calamansi as sauce.
