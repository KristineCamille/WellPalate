AMPALAYA CON CARNE

Ampalaya con carne is a Filipino dish that features finely sliced bitter melon, known as ampalaya, that is famous for its distinct taste and health benefits. The bitterness of the vegetable perfectly complements the savory richness of tender beef strips. With its low-carb profile and the nutritional benefits of bitter melon, ampalaya con carne stands out as a smart choice. Each bite is a textural delight—crisp ampalaya, tender beef, and a medley of spices. Served with rice or as part of a balanced meal, it becomes a delicious and health-conscious choice for those seeking a delightful dining experience with wellness in mind.

INGREDIENTS:

  • ½ kg beef sirloin or pork
  • 1 piece ampalaya
  • 1 onion
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • ¾ cups water
  • Cooking oil
  • 2 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 ½ teaspoon olive oil
  • Small giner
  • Pepper
  • 1 ½ teaspoon cornstarch

PROCEDURES:

  1. Marinate the beef or pork with oyster sauce, soy sauce, pepper, garlic, cornstarch, and olive oil for 10-20 minutes.
  2. Saute the onion, ginger, and remaining garlic. Add the marinated beef or pork. Stir-fry until golden brown or until the meat is tender.
  3. Add the ampalaya and water. Let it simmer for 5-8 minutes or until every ingredient is cooked.
  4. Turn off the heat and enjoy a serving of Ampalaya Con Carne with a cup of rice!

NUTRIENT FACTS:
