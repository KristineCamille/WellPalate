ADOBONG TALONG

Adobong Talong, a Filipino favorite, highlights the delightful combination of eggplants bathed in a savory soy sauce-based marinade. This dish is a great option for those managing diabetes for several reasons. Eggplants, being low in carbohydrates and calories, offer a nutrient-dense choice rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. The soy sauce marinade adds a burst of flavor without substantially affecting blood sugar levels.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 4-5 medium size eggplant
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • ½ cup water
  • 3 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chili peppers (optional)

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a wide pan, heat the oil over medium level. Fry the eggplant until light brown. Drain on paper towels. Set aside half of the oil.
  2. Using the same pan, saute the garlic for 30 seconds
  3. Add the vinegar, water, sugar, and soy sauce. Bring it to boil without stirring to make sure that vinegar is cooked.
  4. Add eggplant and chili peppers. Cook, covered, for about 4 to 5 minutes or until tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  259kcal
Carbohydrates  29g
Protein  6g
Fat 15g
Sodium 822mg
Sugar 17g
