SHRIMP AVOCADO SALAD

Savor the delightful combination of shrimp and creamy avocado in this refreshing salad. Shrimp provides lean protein, while avocado contributes healthy fats, offering a satisfying and heart-healthy element. The inclusion of fresh vegetables adds fiber, vitamins, and minerals without a significant impact on blood sugar levels. Its carefully chosen ingredients make it a smart choice for those mindful of their well-being. Enjoy the goodness of shrimp avocado salad—where flavor meets health in every bite!

INGREDIENTS:

  • ½ to 1 kilogram peeled Shrimp
  • 2-3 Avocados
  • Lettuce
  • ¼ cup red Onion
  • 1 tablespoon Cilantro or Parsley as substitute
  • 3 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 tablespoon lime or calamansi juice
  • 1 teaspoon Paprika or Curry powder
  • Salt and Pepper

PROCEDURES:

  1. Steam the shrimp for six minutes. Set aside.
  2. To make the dressing, combine the olive oil, lime or calamansi juice, paprika or curry powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the lettuce, red onion, cubed avocados, cilantro or parsley, steamed shrimp, and dressing.
  4. Serve at room temperature or chilled.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  363kcal
Carbohydrates  12g
Protein  25g
Fat 26g
Sodium 438mg
Sugar 2 g
