CHICKEN LETTUCE WRAP

Enjoy the goodness of chicken lettuce wraps—a diabetes-friendly treat that features crisp lettuce holding flavorful seasoned chicken. It's a smart and tasty low-carb option, bursting with flavor while keeping health in mind. These wraps aren't just about great taste; they're a savvy option for those watching their carb intake. Packed with protein and vibrant flavors, these chicken lettuce wraps promise a tasty, health-conscious meal.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1kl ground chicken
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon ginger
  • 1 can whole water chestnuts or almond nuts
  • Onion leaves
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lettuce

PROCEDURES:

  1. Add the olive oil to a heated pan. Saute the garlic, onion, ginger, and ground chicken for 8-10 mins.
  2. Add the soy sauce, vinegar, and onion leaves. Occasionally stir for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Mix the water chestnuts or almond nuts, the substitute, and let it cook for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Once cooked, wrap two or three tablespoons of chicken mixture into the lettuce leaf.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  168kcal
Carbohydrates  8g
Protein  20g
Fat 6.2
Sodium 910.2mg
Sugar 2.9g
