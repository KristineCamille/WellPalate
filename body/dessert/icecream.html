CHOCOLATE-COFFEE ICE CREAM

Dive into the tasty blend of Chocolate Coffee Ice Cream. Enjoy the smooth chocolate mixed with a strong coffee kick in every creamy scoop. It's a delightful frozen treat that combines the best of both worlds. Whether it's a warm afternoon or a cozy evening, this simple recipe is here to satisfy your sweet and coffee cravings in one delightful experience. INGREDIENTS:

  • Chilled all purpose cream
  • Chilled condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup instant coffee granules
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together cream until it doubles its size.
  2. Once doubled, gently add the condensed milk, coffee granules, cocoa powder, sugar, and chocolate chips.
  3. Mix the ingredients well.
  4. Transfer to a container and freeze until firm.
