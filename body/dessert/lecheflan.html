LECHE FLAN

Leche flan is a rich and creamy caramel custard dessert that's popular in many Filipino households. With eggs, sweetened condensed milk, and evaporated milk as its magical trio, this treat is a delightful fusion of creamy and sweet goodness. Enjoy it chilled for that set-to-perfection texture or warm for a cozy indulgence ‚Äì either way, it's a treat that never disappoints. So, why wait? INGREDIENTS:

  • 10 egg yolks
  • 1 can (14 oz) condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar (for caramel)
  • 1/4 cup water (for caramel)

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  2. In a saucepan, combine sugar and water for the caramel. Cook over medium heat without stirring until it turns amber in color. Pour the caramel into a round mold.
  3. Pour the egg mixture over the caramel.
  4. Cover the mold with aluminum foil and steam for about 30-40 minutes or until set.
  5. Let it cool, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving. To serve, run a knife around the edge of the mold and invert onto a plate.
