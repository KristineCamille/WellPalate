BUKO PANDAN SALAD

Experience the tropical joy of making Buko Pandan right in your kitchen! With simple steps and a handful of ingredients, you can whip up this Filipino favorite. The coconut's chewiness and the pandan's freshness make every spoonful a delightful treat. Whether for a special occasion or a sunny day, Buko Pandan is your easy ticket to a taste of tropical bliss. INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 cups coconut meat or strips
  • 1 pack pandan-flavored jelly
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose cream
  • Â½ cup tapioca pearls (optional)
  • 1 cup nata de coco (optional)

PROCEDURES:

  1. Cook the pandan-flavored jelly and cut it into cubes.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, mix the coconut and cubed jelly.
  3. Add condensed milk, all-purpose cream, sugar, tapioca pearls (if using), and nata de coco (if using).
  4. Chill in the refrigerator for a refreshing escape to tropical paradise.
