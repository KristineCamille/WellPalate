CREMA DE FRUTA

Crema De Fruta is a carnival of flavors, a Filipino masterpiece that dances on your taste buds! Layer of sponge cake, smooth custard, and sweet fruit cocktail, creating a delicious harmony of flavors and textures.The sponge cake serves as a light and airy foundation, while the custard layer adds a rich, creamy texture that perfectly complements the sweetness of the fruit cocktail. Whether enjoyed cool on a sunny day or as a delightful ending to a special meal, Crema de Fruta stands as a genuine Filipino favorite, ensuring a sweet retreat with each delicious spoonful. INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 sponge cake
  • 1 cup custard
  • 1 cup mixed fruits (like peaches, cherries, and kiwi)
  • 1 pack unflavored gelatin
  • ¬Ω cup sugar
  • ¬Ω cup water

PROCEDURES:

  1. Slice the sponge cake into thin, even layers.
  2. Arrange a layer of sponge cake at the bottom of a serving dish.
  3. Spread a layer of custard over the sponge cake.
  4. Add a layer of mixed fruits.
  5. Repeat the layers until you fill the dish, ending with fruits on top.
  6. Dissolve gelatin in water and sugar, then heat until fully dissolved.
  7. Pour the gelatin mixture over the fruit layer.
  8. Chill in the refrigerator until set.
