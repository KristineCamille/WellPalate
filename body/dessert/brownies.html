BROWNIES

Brownies are classic, fudgy chocolate squares that are a favorite treat for chocolate lovers. A heavenly blend of rich chocolate and buttery goodness in every bite. Whether enjoyed warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or savored alongside a steaming cup of coffee, you'll find yourself lost in the simple joy that only a perfect brownie can provide. So, why resist the temptation? Dive into the delight of the irresistible brownies recipe and make your day a little sweeter. Enjoy every bite! (๑ᵔ⤙ᵔ๑)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

PROCEDURES:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Melt the butter in a saucepan over low heat. Remove from heat and stir in the sugar.
  3. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each. Add the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add to the egg mixture and stir until well combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared dish and spread it evenly.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  7. Allow to cool before cutting into squares.
