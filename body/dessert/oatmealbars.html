PEANUT BUTTER OATMEAL BARS

These No-Bake Peanut Butter Oatmeal Bars are like a burst of sunshine on a cloudy day. Picture creamy peanut butter and hearty oats teaming up, accented with a golden drizzle of honey. These bars are a breeze to make – no oven required. Simply mix your favorite peanut butter with oats, add a drizzle of honey or maple syrup for sweetness, and press the mixture into a pan. Let it chill, and voilà! You've got a batch of irresistible bars that are both wholesome and satisfying. They're your passport to quick and nutritious snacking, ready to save the day!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup peanut butter
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • ½ cup chocolate chips
  • ¼ cup chopped nuts (optional)

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a mixing bowl, unite the oats, peanut butter, and honey in a heroic alliance.
  2. Toss in the chocolate chips and, if you dare, the chopped nuts for an added crunch.
  3. Press this formidable mixture into a lined dish and let it cool its heels in the refrigerator until it firms up.
  4. Summon your inner hero to cut it into bars fit for champions, and let the snacking commence!

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  300 kcal
Carbohydrates  45g
Protein  2g
Fat 3g
Sodium 40mg
Sugar 40g
