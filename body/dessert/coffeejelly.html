COFFEE JELLY

Coffee jelly is a refreshing and delightful dessert that combines the bold flavors of coffee with the silky texture of jelly. You don't need to be a kitchen expert to make this treat; it's a straightforward process with satisfying results.It's made by setting a coffee-infused gelatin mixture and cutting it into cubes. Served with sweetened cream, it's a delightful treat for coffee lovers. So, why not take a break from the ordinary and take coffee to another level? Grab your ingredients, follow these simple steps, and let the aroma of coffee fill your kitchen. ◝(ᵔᵕᵔ)◜

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 packets unflavored gelatin (about 14g)
  • 2 cups brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or all-purpose cream
  • 2 tablespoons condensed milk

PROCEDURES

  1. Dissolve the gelatin in the brewed coffee and let it sit for a few minutes.
  2. Heat the mixture over low heat, stirring until the gelatin completely dissolves. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.
  3. Pour the mixture into a square dish and refrigerate until set (about 4 hours or overnight).
  4. Cut the set jelly into cubes.
  5. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form, then fold in the condensed milk. Serve the coffee jelly cubes with the sweetened cream mixture.
