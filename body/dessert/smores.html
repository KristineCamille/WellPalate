S'MORES BARS

S'mores Bars are a campfire classic, turned into a gooey, chewy marvel! Golden graham crackers, melty chocolate, and pillowy marshmallows unite in a dessert that'll transport you to cozy nights by the fire. It's a perfect combination that captures the essence of a classic S'mores experience in every mouthful. Whether you're reminiscing about camping trips or just craving a delicious treat, these S'mores Bars are your ticket to a cozy, chocolatey, and marshmallow-filled escape. So, gather your ingredients, no need for a campfire, and get ready to enjoy the delightful nostalgia of S'mores in a convenient bar form. Happy snacking!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 3 cups mini marshmallows

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Press this mixture into a lined dish to form the crust.
  2. Sprinkle a layer of chocolate chips over the crust.
  3. Cover the chocolate chips with mini marshmallows, creating a fluffy cloud blanket.
  4. Broil in the oven for a minute or two, keeping a close eye to prevent burning. You want the marshmallows to be golden and gooey.
  5. Allow it to cool before cutting into squares. Each bite is a nostalgic journey around the campfire.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  240 kcal
Carbohydrates  30g
Protein  2g
Fat 17g
Sodium 170mg
Sugar 20g
