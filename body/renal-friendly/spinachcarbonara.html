SPINACH CARBONARA

Spinach Carbonara is a lush, velvety dance of pasta and greens. This indulgent yet health-conscious creation combines al dente pasta with a velvety carbonara sauce infused with fresh spinach. Beyond its rich taste, this dish offers a wealth of benefits. Spinach, a nutritional powerhouse, adds a dose of vitamins and antioxidants while contributing to heart health. The protein from the eggs and the savory notes from Parmesan cheese create a satisfying and balanced meal. The Spinach Carbonara is a delectable journey where wholesome ingredients and delightful flavors come together, making it a tasty and nourishing choice for any occasion.It's a classic Italian indulgence with a healthy twist!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking

PROCEDURES:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together grated Parmesan cheese and eggs. Set aside.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add fresh spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted.
  5. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to combine.
  6. Remove the skillet from heat and quickly pour in the egg mixture. Toss vigorously to coat the pasta.
  7. If the sauce is too thick, add reserved pasta water a little at a time until desired consistency is reached.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot, garnished with extra Parmesan if desired.
