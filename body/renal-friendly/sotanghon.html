CHICKEN SOTANGHON

Chicken Sotanghon is a comforting Filipino noodle soup made with bean thread noodles, chicken, and an assortment of vegetables. The lean protein from chicken supports muscle health, while the vegetables contribute essential nutrients. The clear broth and light nature of sotanghon make it a gentle option for digestion. It's a comforting bowl that not only satisfies your palate but also provides a wholesome and nourishing experience for your overall well-being. Indulge in the warmth and goodness of Chicken Sotanghon, where flavor meets health in every slurp!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 200g bean thread noodles (sotanghon)
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups sliced mixed vegetables (e.g., carrots, cabbage, bell peppers)
  • 6 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt substitute (to taste)
  • Ground black pepper (to taste)

PROCEDURES:

  1. Soak the bean thread noodles in warm water until softened. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat the cooking oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Saute until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  4. Pour in the low-sodium chicken broth and bring to a simmer. Add the mixed vegetables and cook until tender-crisp.
  5. Season with salt substitute and ground black pepper to taste.
  6. Add the soaked and drained bean thread noodles. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  7. Serve hot.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  250 kcal
Carbohydrates  30g
Protein  25g
Fat 3g
Sodium 100mg
Sugar 2g
