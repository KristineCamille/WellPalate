AVOCADO TOAST

Avocado Toast is the epitome of simple, nutritious elegance. Creamy avocado meets crispy toast in a harmonious union of textures. Avocado is a nutrient-dense fruit that brings a burst of flavor without contributing to high levels of potassium or phosphorus, making it a smart choice for those mindful of renal health. Whether you start your day with it, enjoy it as a light lunch, or reach for it as a wholesome snack, this Avocado Toast recipe isn't just a culinary delight; it's a conscious choice for those seeking a balanced and flavorful option. So, relish the rich taste and nourishing benefits in every delightful bite. Happy and mindful eating!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread, toasted
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: poached egg, cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, feta cheese

PROCEDURES:

  1. Halve the avocado, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash with a fork until creamy but still slightly chunky.
  2. Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto the toasted bread slices.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper
  4. Add your choice of optional toppings, if desired.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  250 kcal
Carbohydrates  20g
Protein  5g
Fat 15g
Sodium 382.5mg
Sugar 1.3g
