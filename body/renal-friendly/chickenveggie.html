CHICKEN VEGGIE STIR-FRY

Chicken Veggie Stir-fry is a symphony of colors and flavors. With an array of nutrient-rich veggies, lean protein from chicken, and a boost of fiber, it's a wholesome choice. Packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, this stir-fry not only satisfies your taste buds but also contributes to overall well-being. It's a quick and delicious way to embrace a nutritious meal, promoting digestive health, supporting muscle maintenance, and offering a burst of energy. So, treat yourself to a plate of health-conscious indulgence with our Chicken Veggie Stir-Fry.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger. Set aside.
  2. Heat cooking oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  3. Add chicken slices and cook until no longer pink. Remove and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add more oil if needed. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  5. Return the cooked chicken to the pan and pour the sauce over. Stir-fry for another minute until everything is well-coated and heated through.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  300 kcal
Carbohydrates  15g
Protein  30g
Fat 15g
Sodium 1025.2mg
Sugar 1g
