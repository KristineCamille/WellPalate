TOFU STIR-FRY

Tofu Stir-fry is a celebration of plant-based goodness. This savory medley features tofu, vibrant vegetables, and a flavorful stir-fry sauce. Tofu, being a low-phosphorus and high-quality protein source, makes it a kidney-friendly option. The colorful array of vegetables not only adds crunch and flavor but also provides essential vitamins and antioxidants. It's a wholesome, flavorful dish that's bound to satisfy your taste buds!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 block firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (e.g., broccoli, bell peppers, snap peas)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Green onions for garnish
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger. Set aside.
  2. Heat cooking oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  3. Add tofu cubes and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add more oil if needed. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  5. Return the cooked tofu to the pan and pour the sauce over. Stir-fry for another minute until everything is well-coated and heated through.
  6. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds before serving.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  250 kcal
Carbohydrates  15g
Protein  15g
Fat 15g
Sodium 51.2mg
Sugar 2.8g
