MEDITERRANEAN TUNA SALAD

Mediterranean Tuna Salad is a refreshing a refreshing blend of high-quality tuna, olives, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and feta cheese, all kissed with a zesty olive oil and lemon dressing. Beyond its delightful taste, this salad offers a wealth of health benefits. Rich in omega-3 fatty acids, lean protein, antioxidants, and heart-healthy fats, it's a delicious way to support your overall well-being. Indulge in a plate of Mediterranean goodness that not only satisfies your palate but also nourishes your body. Enjoy the fusion of flavors and health benefits with our Tuna Salad!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 cans tuna, drained
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a large bowl, combine tuna, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, olives, and red onion.
  2. Drizzle lemon juice and olive oil over the salad. Toss gently to combine.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  250 kcal
Carbohydrates  8g
Protein  25g
Fat 14g
Sodium 216mg
Sugar 1g
