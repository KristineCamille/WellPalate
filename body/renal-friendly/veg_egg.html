VEGETABLE AND EGG SCRAMBLE

This dish is a simple and nutritious scramble of eggs and an assortment of kidney-friendly vegetables. Picture vibrant, crisp vegetables like bell peppers, tomatoes, and spinach mingling with fluffy scrambled eggs. Beyond its delicious taste, this dish offers a multitude of health benefits. Packed with essential nutrients, the vegetables provide vitamins and antioxidants, while the eggs offer a protein boost for sustained energy. Experience a burst of morning vitality with THIS Vegetable and Egg Scramble recipe, a wholesome and energizing breakfast option.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (e.g., spinach, bell peppers, zucchini), chopped
  • 1/4 cup low-fat cheese, shredded (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons low-fat milk
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, low-fat milk, salt substitute, and pepper.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped vegetables and sauté until tender.
  3. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables and cook, stirring gently, until the eggs are just set.
  4. If using, sprinkle the low-fat cheese over the top and let it melt slightly.
  5. Serve hot.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  200 kcal
Carbohydrates  4g
Protein  14g
Fat 15g
Sodium 150mg
Sugar 4g
