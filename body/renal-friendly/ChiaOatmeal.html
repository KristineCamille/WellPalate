CHIA OATMEAL MIX

Chia oatmeal mix is a nutritious and kidney-friendly breakfast option. Oats are a fantastic source of soluble fiber, providing a heart-healthy option while being gentle on the kidneys. Chia seeds, in turn, add a boost of nutrition without contributing to high levels of potassium or phosphorus, making them a kidney-friendly food. This recipe also offers a perfect balance of flavors and textures, from comforting creaminess of oats to the delightful crunch of chia seeds. So, grab a spoon, dive in, and relish the warmth, taste, and nourishment that this Chia Oatmeal Mix brings to your table. Here's to happy and healthy eating!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups low-fat milk
  • 2 tablespoons honey (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Fresh berries (for topping, optional)

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chia seeds, rolled oats, low-fat milk, honey (if using), and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  2. Cover and refrigerate the mixture for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  3. Stir well before serving. Add a little more milk if needed to reach desired consistency.
  4. Top with fresh berries, if desired.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  270 kcal
Carbohydrates  40g
Protein  9g
Fat 8g
Sodium 70mg
Sugar 8g
