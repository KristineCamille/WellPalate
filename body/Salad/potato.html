POTATO SALAD

Potato salad is a classic side dish that's hearty and full of flavor. It features tender potatoes, crunchy celery, and zesty red onions, all coated in a creamy mayonnaise dressing. Potato Salad is a versatile star that shines in various settings. Whether it's stealing the show at a summer barbecue, adding a touch of class to a picnic spread, or becoming a beloved side dish at family dinners, its adaptability makes it a cherished favorite.The taste is comforting, familiar, and undeniably delicious – a kitchen journey that brings people together. otato Salad is a timeless delight that satisfies the palate and warms the heart. Enjoy the creamy, crunchy, and flavorful experience!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 4 cups potatoes, boiled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup celery, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley (optional, for garnish)

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the boiled and cubed potatoes, chopped celery, and finely chopped red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. Gently fold the dressing into the potato mixture until well coated.
  4. Garnish with chopped parsley (if desired) and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
