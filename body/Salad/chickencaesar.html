CHICKEN CAESAR SALAD

Chicken Caesar salad is a refreshing and satisfying dish that effortlessly balances flavors and nutrition. Start with fresh lettuce leaves, add chicken strips for a protein boost, and drizzle the ensemble with creamy Caesar dressing. Toss it all together, and you have a classic salad that's both hearty and delicious.Beyond its ease of preparation, Chicken Caesar salad brings a range of health benefits to the table. The fresh vegetables provide essential vitamins and minerals, while the lean protein from the chicken supports muscle health. The dressing, when made with mindful choices, contributes to a satisfying meal without compromising on taste. So, when you're craving a simple, nutritious, and delicious meal, turn to the goodness of Chicken Caesar salad.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 to 3 chicken breasts
  • Lettuce
  • Pack croutons or wheat bread as substitute
  • ¾ cup Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • ¾ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon calamansi juice
  • 1-2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce or oyster sauce as substitute
  • 1-2 teaspoon mustard
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a small bowl, mix the mayonnaise, half of the Parmesan cheese, calamansi juice, Worcestershire sauce or oyster sauce, mustard, salt, and pepper.
  2. In a frying pan, cook the chicken breast with olive oil or butter until golden brown.
  3. In a separate large pan, heat an oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and wait until it is medium brown and add the croutons or small pieces of wheat bread. Stir and fry until golden brown or crispy.
  4. In a large bowl, place the lettuce, croutons or wheat bread, and dressing mixture. Mix well and top with the remaining parmesan cheese
