TUNA SALAD

Tuna salad is a protein-packed dish that's both nutritious and satisfying. It features flaked tuna, crunchy celery, red onion, and creamy mayonnaise. Whether you enjoy it on a sandwich, as a refreshing side, or in a wrap, this recipe is a burst of flavors that caters to your cravings for something savory and satisfying. It's not just a salad; it's a taste adventure that keeps you coming back for more. Happy savoring!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 cans tuna, drained and flaked
  • 1/4 cup celery, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lettuce leaves (optional, for serving)

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a bowl, combine the flaked tuna, chopped celery, and chopped red onion.
  2. Add mayonnaise and lemon juice, then mix until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Serve chilled on a bed of lettuce leaves, if desired.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  220 kcal
Carbohydrates  2g(1%)
Protein  28g(56%)
Fat 11g(17%)
Sodium 300mg(13%)
Sugar 1g(1%)
