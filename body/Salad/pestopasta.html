PESTO PASTA SALAD

Pesto Pasta Salad is a delightful dish where perfectly cooked pasta meets a flavorful basil pesto sauce and fresh, colorful veggies. This is not just a treat for your taste buds; it's a nutritious choice too. Basil, a key ingredient in pesto, brings anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties to the table. Olive oil in the pesto offers heart-healthy fats, and the veggies contribute essential vitamins and minerals. Elevate your dining experience and savor the joy of delicious Pesto Pasta Salad, paired with its healthy goodness

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 kilogram Spiral pasta
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • ½ to 1 cup mozzarella cheese
For pesto sauce:
  • 2 cups basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons pine nut or walnuts for substitute
  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • ½ cup parmesan cheese

PROCEDURES:

  1. Boil the pasta until cooked and softened. Drain and rinse the noodles with water. Set aside.
  2. For preparing the sauce, blender the nuts, basil, garlic, and parmesan cheese.
  3. Once all ingredients are crushed, slowly add the olive oil while the blender is at slow speed or pulsing.
  4. Add the cherry tomato to the pasta and mix. Pour the pesto sauce and stir well.
  5. Top with mozzarella cheese and enjoy!

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Serving Size: 150 grams

Calories  258 kcal
Carbohydrates  33g
Protein  7.1g
Fat 10.1g
Sodium 700mg
Sugar 0.8g
