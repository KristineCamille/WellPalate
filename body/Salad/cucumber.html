CUCUMBER SALAD

Discover the goodness of cucumber salad—a simple, cool dish that's all about fresh crunch. With a touch of red onions, seasonings, and vinegar, it's a burst of flavors that's easy to enjoy. The cucumbers provide a cool and crunchy base, while the red onions add a hint of sharpness, and the burst of sweetness from the optional cherry tomatoes completes the ensemble. Whether served as a side or enjoyed on its own, cucumber salad adds a refreshing touch to your table. It's the perfect side dish for any occasion, bringing a touch of simplicity and refreshing goodness to your table. Happy crunching!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 pieces cucumber
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1 piece red onion
  • ½ tablespoon sugar
  • ¼ cup apple cider vinegar or white vinegar for substitute

PROCEDURES:

  1. Thinly slice the cucumbers and red onion.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced cucumber and red onion, salt, pepper, sugar, and vinegar. Stir it well.
  3. Refrigerate for 30 mins to achieve the best taste.
