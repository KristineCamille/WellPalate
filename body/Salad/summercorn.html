SUMMER CORN SALAD

Savor the essence of summer with refreshing Summer Corn Salad—a colorful medley of sweet corn, avocados, crisp bell peppers, and tasty dressing. This dish is easy to prepare; there's no need for you to use your stove. Just grab your apron, mixing bowls, spoons, and you're good to go. This vibrant salad not only delights your taste buds with the sweet crunch of corn and the creamy richness of avocados but also offers a hassle-free preparation that lets you enjoy the flavors of summer with minimal effort.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 5-7 corns or 2 cans of corn kernels
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ cup red onion
  • ½ cup red bell pepper
  • 1 avocado
For dressing:
  • 4 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 2 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoon mustard
  • 1-2 calamansi
  • Salt and pepper

PROCEDURES:

  1. Prepare the dressing in a small bowl by combining the olive oil, apple cider, sugar, mustard, calamansi juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the cubed avocado, corn kernels, bell peppers, onions, and olive oil.
  3. Pour in the dressing and stir gently. Chill for 30 minutes to 1 hour and it's ready to serve.
