SPINACH SALAD

Spinach salad is a refreshing and nutritious dish packed with vitamins and minerals. Fresh spinach leaves are combined with vibrant vegetables and a tangy vinaigrette dressing, creating a delightful burst of flavors. Let's explore the flavors of this Spinach Salad recipe. The spinach provides a crisp and earthy base, and the tomatoes bring a burst of juicy sweetness. The onions contribute a subtle sharpness, while the cheese adds a creamy delight that harmoniously balances the combination. Each bite is a delightful fusion of textures and flavors that gracefully unfolds on your palate. This salad is a perfect accompaniment to any meal or can be enjoyed on its own for a light and healthy lunch option.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 4 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 cup tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the fresh spinach leaves, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and feta cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients evenly.
  4. Serve immediately.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  180 kcal
Carbohydrates  12g
Protein  7g
Fat 14g
Sodium 310mg
Sugar 4g
