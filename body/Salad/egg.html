EGG SALAD

Egg salad is a classic and satisfying dish that celebrates the simplicity of ingredients. It's made with hard-boiled eggs, creamy mayonnaise, tangy mustard, and a touch of fresh herbs. Whether you scoop it onto a bed of fresh greens, spread it between slices of bread, or enjoy it on its own, Egg Salad is a versatile delight. It's the kind of dish that transports you back to the simple joys of home-cooked meals and picnics in the park. So, whether you're a longtime fan of Egg Salad or new to the experience, get ready for a bite that's both timeless and comforting.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs, peeled and chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chives, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Paprika for garnish (optional)

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chopped hard-boiled eggs, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and fresh chives.
  2. Mix until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Optionally, sprinkle paprika on top for a pop of color and flavor.
  4. Serve chilled on a bed of lettuce or as a sandwich filling.
