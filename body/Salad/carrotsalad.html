CARROT SALAD

Dive into the goodness of the easy-peasy carrot salad! The ease of making carrot salad lies in its minimal preparation – a quick peel and grate, and you're well on your way to a delightful salad creation. To assemble this salad, simply toss the grated carrots with a light dressing. Enhance the flavors with optional additions like raisins, nuts, or fresh fruits for added texture and depth. So, if you're in the mood for a simple, nutritious, and downright delicious creation, carrot salad is here to steal the show. Fresh, flavorful, and ready in a snap – it's a colorful journey to health and taste that you won't want to miss!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2-4 cups of julienne grated Carrots
  • ½ cup raisins
  • 1 large apple
  • ¼ to ½ cup of mayonnaise

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the carrots, raisin, sliced apple, and mayonnaise.
  2. Mix well and refrigerate for an hour for best taste.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Serving Size: 200 grams

Protein  4g
