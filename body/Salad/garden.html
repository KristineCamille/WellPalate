GARDEN SALAD

Garden salad is a vibrant medley of fresh vegetables, making it a healthy and colorful addition to any meal. It features crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers, and red onions, all tossed in a light dressing. Each forkful is a journey through a flourishing garden, where the crunch of veggies meets the burst of dressing, creating a symphony of freshness. Garden Salad is your ticket to a simple, delicious, and vibrant meal. Happy eating!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 4 cups mixed lettuce leaves (e.g., romaine, arugula, spinach)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  2. In a saucepan, combine sugar and water for the caramel. Cook over medium heat without stirring until it turns amber in color. Pour the caramel into a round mold.
  3. Pour the egg mixture over the caramel.
  4. Cover the mold with aluminum foil and steam for about 30-40 minutes or until set.
  5. Let it cool, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving. To serve, run a knife around the edge of the mold and invert onto a plate.
