CREAMY FETTUCCINE MUSHROOMS

Creamy Fettuccine Mushrooms is a velvety pasta delight. Silken ribbons of fettuccine dance with earthy mushrooms in a decadent cream sauce. Mushrooms, packed with essential nutrients, bring a dose of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants to the table. The creamy sauce, when made mindfully, can offer a balance of richness without overwhelming the dish with excessive saturated fats. It's a dish that marries comfort and sophistication, making every bite a luxurious experience.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

PROCEDURES:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced mushrooms and cook until golden brown and tender.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Cook for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and toss to coat in the creamy mushroom sauce.
  6. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving

NUTRIENT FACTS:
