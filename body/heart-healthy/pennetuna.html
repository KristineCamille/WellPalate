ITALIAN PENNE WITH TUNA

Italian Penne with Tuna is a quick and satisfying pasta dish that combines the heartiness of tuna with the zing of Italian flavors. Tuna, a lean protein source, provides essential amino acids for muscle health, while the whole-grain penne adds fiber for digestive well-being. The Mediterranean-inspired ingredients contribute to heart health, and the olive oil-based sauce offers healthy fats. With a balance of taste and nutrition, Italian Penne with Tuna is a delicious choice that supports overall wellness. It's a go-to recipe for busy days when you want a delicious meal on the table in no time.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 8 oz penne pasta
  • 2 cans tuna in olive oil, drained
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

PROCEDURES:

  1. Cook the penne pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add sun-dried tomatoes and tuna to the skillet. Cook for a few minutes to heat through.
  4. Stir in capers, lemon juice, and chopped parsley.
  5. Add the cooked penne to the skillet and toss to combine.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  450 kcal
Carbohydrates  40g
Protein  25g
Fat 20g
Sodium 422mg
Sugar 6g
