STIR-FRY SHRIMP AND MUSHROOMS

Treat your taste buds and your heart with stir-fried shrimp and mushrooms! It's a good choice if you're watching your blood pressure. With its low-sodium approach to stir-frying, the dish maintains a delicate balance, enhanced by the aromatic fusion of garlic, ginger, and butter. Plus, it has things called antioxidants that are good for your heart. It's quick, tasty, and a smart pick for your health. Enjoy it over rice or noodles for a yummy and heart-friendly meal!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 500 g shrimp
  • 250-300 g mushrooms
  • 5 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Paprika
  • 5 Garlic cloves
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 tablespoon parsley
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon calamansi juice

PROCEDURES:

  • Peel the shrimp or you can buy peeled shrimp for less hassle.
  • In a large pan, saute the garlic and onion with olive oil for 1 minute. Add the shrimp and wait for it to be golden brown.
  • After 5-7 minutes, add the mushroom, parsley, butter, and calamansi juice.
  • After a minute, add the paprika, salt, and pepper to taste. Before turning off the heat, kindly check if the chicken is tender enough.
  • Once all ingredients are cooked, serve and enjoy with pasta or rice.
