FISH CARDILLO

Fish cardillo typically involves frying fish until golden brown and then topping it with flavorful sauce made from tomatoes, onions, garlic, and eggs. Rich in flavor and low in sodium, it's a savory symphony that's not only delicious but also perfect for those with hypertension. The tomatoes and other spices contribute to a nutritious and well-balanced meal. Cook with joy, and nourish your body with the delicious benefits of fish cardillo!

INGREDIENTS:

  • Tilapia or any fish you prefer
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 to 1 cup water

PROCEDURES:

  1. Pan fry the fish over medium heat. Occasionally turn to reach the perfect golden brown color.
  2. Once cooked, transfer the fish to a clean plate and remove any excess oil.
  3. Using the same pan, saute the garlic, onion, and tomatoes.
  4. Put in the fried fish and add water. Once it boils, add the beaten eggs and season with salt and pepper.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  170g
Carbohydrates  13g
Protein  20g
Fat 4g
Sodium 115mg
Sugar 26mg
