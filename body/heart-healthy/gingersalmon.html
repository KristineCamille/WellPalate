GINGER SALMON

Ginger Salmon is a symphony of bold flavors. Succulent salmon fillets marinate in a zesty ginger sauce, creating a dish that's as vibrant as it is delicious. This culinary masterpiece not only delights the taste buds but also offers an array of health benefits. Ginger, with its anti-inflammatory properties, adds a zesty kick while potentially aiding digestion. Salmon, a fatty fish rich in omega-3 fatty acids, contributes to heart health and supports brain function. Together, these ingredients create a symphony of taste and wellness, making our Ginger and Salmon dish a delicious and nutritious choice for those seeking a flavorful journey with added health benefits. Enjoy a plate that's as good for your palate as it is for your well-being!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 salmon filets
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, grated ginger, honey, minced garlic, and olive oil.
  2. Place the salmon filets in a dish and pour the marinade over them. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the marinated salmon on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake for about 15-20 minutes or until the salmon is cooked to your liking.
  5. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve.

NUTRIENT FACTS:

Calories  300 kcal
Carbohydrates  8g
Protein  20g
Fat 18g
Sodium 140.4mg
Sugar 4.3g
