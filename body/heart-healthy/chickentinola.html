CHICKEN TINOLA

Chicken Tinola is a Filipino comfort soup that warms the heart and nourishes the soul.The ginger adds a warm, soothing touch while potentially aiding digestion. The lean protein from the chicken supports muscle health, and the nutrient-rich vegetables ontribute essential vitamins and minerals. With its clear and flavorful broth, Chicken Tinola is a go-to comfort food that not only delights your taste buds but also nourishes your body. Dive into a bowlful of tradition and wellness with each steaming spoonful!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 500g chicken pieces (legs or thighs)
  • 1 small green papaya, peeled, seeds removed, and cut into chunks
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (patis)
  • 6 cups water
  • 2 cups spinach leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a pot, heat some oil and sauté ginger, garlic, and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in fish sauce and let it cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add water and bring to a boil. Simmer until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Add green papaya and cook until tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Just before serving, add spinach leaves and let them wilt in the hot soup.
