CHICKEN NUGGETS

Homemade chicken nuggets are much healthier and tastier than frozen or fast-food varieties and they are easy to prepare too. The process involves coating bite-sized chicken pieces in a seasoned mixture, ensuring a perfect balance of flavors. Whether you choose to bake or fry them, the ease of preparation means you can have a batch ready in no time. This recipe will surely be loved by your kids. So, for a quick, tasty, and rewarding kitchen experience homemade chicken nuggets are the ideal choice. PROCEDURES:

  1. Place the flour in a resealable plastic bag and season with the garlic, salt, and pepper. Place the chicken pieces in the bag with the flour and toss to coa
  2. Prepare two medium bowls, one for egg and the other is for melted butter.
  3. Once the chicken is coated with flour, dip it first to the butter, then to the egg, and to the breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat the oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and brown on each side, about 8 minutes total.
  5. Serve and enjoy with the sauce of your choice.
