SIZZLING SISIG

Sizzling sisig is a delicious Filipino favorite dish that is crafted from finely chopped pork belly and cooked until crispy. The mixture of margarine, soy sauce, peppers, and garlic give the dish a real flavor adventure. Served hot on a sizzling plate, it's a feast for the senses—with sizzling sounds and tempting smells. This recipe is easy to prepare, so get your aprons and start the journey!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 ½ kg pork belly
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 pieces white onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 3-4 pieces chili pepper
  • 3 pieces green pepper
  • Ground pepper
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2-3 tablespoon margarine
  • Cooking oil

PROCEDURES:

  1. Boil the pork belly until it is tender.
  2. Dry the boiled pork belly and deep fry in a hot oil. Let it reach its peak, golden brown and crispy. Chopped into small pieces.
  3. Saute the onion and garlic with margarine. Add the pork.
  4. After 1-2 minutes, add the green and chili peppers , soy sauce, ground pepper, and mayonnaise. Stir and taste to make adjustments.
  5. Heat a sizzling plate on a stove top. Melt the margarine and transfer the pork sisig
  6. Serve with egg and rice. Optionally, dress the sisig with mayonnaise on top again.

NUTRIENT FACTS:
