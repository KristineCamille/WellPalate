SWEET AND SOUR CHICKEN

Dive into the delicious world of Sweet and Sour! Imagine tasty bites of chicken glazed with the perfect mix of sweet and tangy goodness. It's an easy yet impressive dish that will make your taste buds dance. The sweetness, derived from a harmonious fusion of sugar or honey, is perfectly counterbalanced by the zing of vinegar or citrus, creating a taste that's both vibrant and satisfying. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a home cook, get ready to impress – you've got this! Happy cooking!. (っ˘ڡ˘ς)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 pound chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch chunks
  • 1 large egg
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 can pineaaple chunks, drained
  • ¼ cup juice from the canned pineapple
  • ¼ cup of water
  • ¼ cup white vinegar
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 4 teaspoons cooking oil
  • Onion and garlic
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger

PROCEDURES:

  1. Combine water, cornstarch, reserved pineapple juice, sugar, ketchup, and vinegar in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium heat; set aside
  2. In a separate pan, saute onion, garlic, and ginger. After 1-2 minutes, add the chicken breast.
  3. Once the chicken is golden brown, add the pineapple chunks and bell peppers. Stir occasionally.
  4. Add the sauce mixture, and salt and pepper to taste. Let it simmer over medium heat.
  5. Once everything is cooked, serve and enjoy over a cup of rice.
