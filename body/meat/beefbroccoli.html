BEEF BROCCOLI

Beef broccoli is a savory and protein rich dish that is often stir-fried to perfection. The harmonious blend of soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and oyster sauce make each bite full of savory goodness. Often served over a cup of rice, beef broccoli embodies a delightful fusion of protein and vegetables, making it a favorite in many Asian-inspired cuisines. Simple to make yet impressively satisfying, it's perfect for both beginners and seasoned cooks.   • 1-2 broccoli
  • 2 teaspoons ginger
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Â¼ cup of water
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and ground pepper
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
For marinade sauce:
  • Â¼ cup oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon liquid seasoning
  • Â½ teaspoon sesame oil or olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

PROCEDURES:

  1. Marinate the beef with oyster sauce, liquid seasoning, oil, and sugar. Add the cornstarch and mix well. Let it sit for 20-40 minutes.
  2. For broccoli, separate it into pieces and slice the hard part thinly.
  3. After the beef is marinated, saute the ginger, garlic, and onion. Once medium brown or a bit caramelized, add the beef.
  4. Stir-fry for 20 to 30 mins or wait until the beef is soft.
  5. Add water, oyster sauce, and pepper. Let it boil for another 5 minutes.
  6. If the beef is tender enough, add the pieces of broccoli and let it cook. It's up to you if the broccoli is half-cooked or not.
  7. Simmer for 3-7 minutes with low to medium heat. Turn off the stove and you're ready to eat!
