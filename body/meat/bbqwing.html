BBQ WINGS

Start your cooking journey with this easy to follow recipe of BBQ Wings! The process is simpler than you think; just pour that rich barbecue sauce into a bowl, mix in the fried chicken, and witness the magic unfold. With each bite, you'll experience the satisfying crunch of the exterior giving way to the juicy meat, perfectly infused with the rich taste of the barbecue glaze. So, roll up your sleeves and embark on a flavorful adventure – BBQ wings await, and your kitchen is the stage for this delicious meal! (｡˃ ᵕ ˂ )⸝ ♨

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 kilogram chicken wings
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Ground pepper
  • Cooking oil
  • ½ to 1 cup BBQ Sauce

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a large ball, season the chicken with salt and pepper
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, cornstarch, baking powder, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. Coat the seasoned chicken with the flour mixture.
  4. Deep-fry the chickens for 10-13 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. Pour the barbeque sauce into a large bowl, mix the fried chicken into the sauce, and it's ready to serve.
