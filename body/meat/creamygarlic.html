CREAMY GARLIC CHICKEN

Creamy garlic chicken is a delicious dish and great comfort food for everyone. The chicken is typically seasoned with salt and pepper, pan-fried until it achieves a golden-brown perfection. Complemented with the creamy garlic sauce, perfect blend of milk, cheese, chicken seasoning, and garlic, that provides mouthwatering and savory taste. This recipe is good for 4 servings and can be easily prepared within 30-45 minutes. So, dive into the kitchen, put on your favorite tunes, and let the aroma of creamy garlic chicken become the soundtrack of your cooking adventure. Happy cooking! (っ˘ڡ˘ς)

INGREDIENTS:

  • Chicken Breast
  • 1 Chicken Cube
  • 1/2 cup cheese
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup onion
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 3 tablespoon butter
  • Ground black pepper
  • Salt

PROCEDURES:

  1. Season the chicken breast with salt and pepper
  2. After 30-40 minutes, toss the chicken to the flour mixed with cheese
  3. Pan fry the chicken until golden brown using butter
  4. For creamy sauce, saute onion and garlic. Add the chicken cube together with the milk, flour, cheese, salt to taste, and pepper. Let it boil.
  5. Once the sauce is thick, add the chicken breast and turn off the heat
