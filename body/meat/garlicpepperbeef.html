GARLIC PEPPER BEEF

Buttery and filled with lots of savory flavors, Garlic Pepper Beef makes for a great lunch or dinner. The dish is characterized by the bold and aromatic presence of minced garlic, infusing each bite with a deep, savory essence. It's a taste experience that manages to be both comforting and exciting, making garlic pepper beef a flavorful journey for the palate, offering a perfect balance of savory, garlicky, and peppery notes. With garlic pepper beef, every stir of the pan is a step toward cooking success—making the kitchen your canvas and this dish your masterpiece. Prepare to enjoy the satisfying results of your cooking efforts! ( ๑ ˃̵ᴗ˂̵)و ♡

INGREDIENTS:

  • 0.25 lb Beef sirloin
  • 1-2 garlic head
  • 1 Knorr beef cube
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 tablespoons canola oil
Marinade Ingredients:
  • 3 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • ½ to 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 3 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

PROCEDURES:

  1. Marinate the beef sirloin with soy sauce, oyster sauce, ground black pepper, garlic powder, and cornstarch.
  2. Toast the minced garlic and set aside
  3. Pour the garlic oil in the pan used earlier. Pan fry the marinated beef until tender. Set aside.
  4. And then add butter to the pan, and put half of Knorr beef cube. Once melted, put the sauteed/fried beef, and stir-fry everything for a good 30 seconds.
  5. Add water and the sauce from the marinade. Wait until it boils over a medium heat. Once cooked, turn off the heat and enjoy!
