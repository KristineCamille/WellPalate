GRILLED PORK BELLY

Grilled Pork Belly is a simple dish that you can easily make with just three steps. The magic happens on the grill, where the pork sizzles, getting a beautiful caramelized coating and those appealing grill marks. Whether you enjoy it as it is, with some sides, or in a sandwich, grilled pork belly turns any meal into a delightful treat. INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 kilogram pork belly
  • ¬Ω to 1 cup soy sauce
  • 5 calamansi
  • ¬Ω to 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • ¬Ω tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoon sriracha sauce or hot sauce

PROCEDURES:

  1. In a large bowl, marinate the pork belly with soy sauce, calamansi juice, ground black pepper, garlic powder, and hot sauce for 1 to 2 hours.
  2. Heat-up the grill. Start grilling the marinated pork belly for 3-5 minutes per side and flip it over and over to prevent getting burnt quickly.
  3. Grill until pork belly is cooked. Cut into small pieces, serve, and enjoy!
