CHICKEN KATSU

Chicken Katsu is a classic Japanese-style fried chickenm featuring a tender chicken coated in a crispy layer of breadcrumbs. It is usually served with flavorful Katsu sauce and cabbage. Chicken Katsu is not only known for its delicious taste but also for its versatility. It can be enjoyed on its own as a main dish, sliced and placed over rice, or even used as a filling for sandwiches or wraps. The simplicity of its preparation makes it a favorite for home cooks and a popular choice on the menus of Japanese restaurants around the world.This recipe is surprisingly easy to make and is sure to be loved by your family! INGREDIENTS:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • 1 cup All-purpose flour
  • 1-2 medium eggs
  • 1 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying
For Katsu Sauce:
  • 1/2 ketchup
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce or Fish sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Sugar

PROCEDURES:

  1. Season the chicken breast with salt, garlic powder, and pepper.
  2. Place the flour and breadcrumbs seperately, in a bowl or wide plate. Beat the eggs in a bowl.
  3. Dip the seasoned chicken in all-purpose flour until it is well coated. Next is to dip it on beaten egg and in breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat a pan with oil. Fry the chicken until golden briwn.
  5. For sauce, simply mix all ingredients in a bowl
  6. You can optionally serve with cabbage and enjoy the chicken katsu with sauce.
